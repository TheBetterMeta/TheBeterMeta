Matches Per Minute

April 22, 2017

I got super bored waiting in the competitive queue the other night, so I decided to figure out when is the best time to play. I’ve added a new chart, Matches Per Minute that gives the average number of competitive matches that happen in one minute throughout the day. It’s broken down by region, so you can look at the data that’s relevant for you.
