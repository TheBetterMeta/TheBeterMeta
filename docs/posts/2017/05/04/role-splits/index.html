First Look at Team Composition

May 4, 2017

With the newest Role Splits chart, I’ve taken our first step toward analyzing team composition. It gives a simple breakdown of how many of each role is viable in competitive play.

On first posting, it looks like flanks are seriously underpowered. If you’re considering between flank and something else… go something else. It’s also hard to go wrong with another tank, no matter how many you already have.
